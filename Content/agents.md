# Situation

It's 11:47 PM on a Tuesday when every light in the city goes out simultaneously.
No warning, no flicker — just total, instant darkness.
Car alarms go off for a few minutes, then fall silent.
No emergency broadcasts, no backup generators, no signal on any phone.
Each person is wherever they happened to be when it happened —
at home, out late, passing through the neighbourhood.
Nobody knows yet whether this is a blown transformer, a cyberattack, or something worse.
Nobody knows how long it will last.
